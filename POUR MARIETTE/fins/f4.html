I feel my body slowly freezing, the mark on my back burning, my breathing ragged and my mind fogging.

I thought...I thought that was the right thing to do.

...

All my senses disappeared...my body froze totally.

She became part of the scenery, a statue in her own imagination. Sad apple trees grew around her, the only element where the gray disrupted the mixture of their colors, a bitter symbol of fatality.
