After answering "yes" to his last, peculiar question, I close my eyes in sudden tiredness and a warmth envelops me. I think back on my life, his questions, this strange but comforting world. "Do I accept real life, in its worst and best moments?" It sounds like an unhappy marriage...but I can't push it away forever, so yes.

So, opening my eyes again, I found my world so tinged with gray. The ice of the lake I had stumbled upon had melted, giving me back the atmosphere of autumn.

As I stand up, something falls to the floor. I pick it up curiously and, looking at it, smile sadly. A hair clip in the shape of a deer.
